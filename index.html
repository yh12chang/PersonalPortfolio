PROJECTS

SoleMate is a start-up project that began in the Summer of 2020 with a group of engineering students at UIUC. Our start-up design is geared towards those who are shoe enthusiasts and wish to maintain their shoe's prime conditions. Our purpose for this project is to design a device that cleans shoes daily. As the concerns of COVID-19 increased, the scope of the project was focused on the ability to sanitize shoes after a day at work.

Currently, the team consists of YongHun Chang (myself) and Wonjoon Lee, who is an electrical engineering major at UIUC and the creator of this start-up. Our start-up is supported by the Zero2One incubator on campus.

Further updates for this start-up will be uploaded onto the SoleMate website.
